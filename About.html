Our Story

How we started and where we are today

Youv.Biz began in 2025 with a simple observation of a huge gap between Manufacturers/Importers and the Distribution ecosystem. There are Manufacturers/Importers who want to expand business throughout the country but they don't have resources to connect with Distributors. At the same time there are Distributors who want to add more products in their portfolio to expand business. Even there are Potential retailers who want to step into wholesale business but companies don't approach them.

With Youv.Biz, manufacturers can reach each and every potential Distributor in big cities and even in Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities with a very simple process.

Founded by a team of Industry veterans and tech innovators, we set out to build a digital bridge. Today, we are proud to facilitate businesses across India to build better, faster and cheaper.
