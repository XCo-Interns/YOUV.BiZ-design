Product Description

Our 18mm Marine Grade Plywood is manufactured using high-quality hardwood timber bonded with BWP grade synthetic resin, with special preservation treatment for borer and termite resistance.

Ideal for kitchens, bathrooms, and high-humidity areas. Calibrated for uniform thickness with a smooth finish for laminating or painting.

  • Boiling Water Proof (BWP) certified
  • Termite and Borer resistant
  • High nail holding strength
  • Zero gap and overlap
